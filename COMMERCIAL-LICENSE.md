# Commercial License

`ormod-rcon` is released under the [PolyForm Perimeter 1.0.0](LICENSE) license. That license permits any use **except** offering a product that competes with ORMOD RCON — for example, running it as a hosted service or reselling it as a managed offering.

If your intended use falls within the competing-use restriction, a separate commercial license is required.

## Who needs a commercial license

You likely need one if you want to:

- Operate a paid hosting service that provides `ormod-rcon` (or a derivative) to third parties
- Bundle `ormod-rcon` into a managed product you sell to others
- Whitelabel or rebrand the dashboard as part of a commercial offering
- Build a product that functionally substitutes for `ormod-rcon` using its code

## Who does NOT need a commercial license

The Perimeter license already permits these without any additional agreement:

- Running `ormod-rcon` for your own community (including paid member tiers, VIP access, donations)
- Using it inside a company, non-profit, school, or government
- Modifying it for your own use
- Paid configuration, setup, or consulting work done *for* a community running their own instance

## How to request a commercial license

Email **[licensing@ormod.gg](mailto:licensing@ormod.gg)** with:

- Your company / organization name
- The intended use case (what you plan to build or offer)
- Approximate scale (users, servers, revenue model)

Commercial terms are negotiated case-by-case. There is no public pricing page.

## Questions about scope

If you are unsure whether your use case falls under the Perimeter license or requires a commercial license, email the same address. A quick clarification costs nothing and is better than guessing.
